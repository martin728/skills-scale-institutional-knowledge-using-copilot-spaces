## Stakeholder Groups

### Executive Leadership
- **Audience:** C-level, VP-level stakeholders
- **Frequency:** Monthly or at major milestones
- **Content:** Executive summary, business impact, risks and mitigation
- **Owner:** Project Sponsor / Product Lead

### Cross-functional Team
- **Audience:** Engineering, Product, Design, QA
- **Frequency:** Weekly
- **Content:** Progress, blockers, dependencies, next steps
- **Owner:** Project Manager

### Business & Product Team
- **Audience:** Product Manager, Business Analysts
- **Frequency:** Bi-weekly or weekly
- **Content:** Feature progress, user feedback, metrics
- **Owner:** Product Manager

### End Users / Support Team
- **Audience:** Customer support, success managers
- **Frequency:** Pre-release and post-release
- **Content:** Release notes, known issues, FAQ
- **Owner:** Communications & Release Manager
